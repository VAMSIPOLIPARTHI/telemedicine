Telemedicine is the use of technology to provide healthcare services remotely. It allows patients to consult with doctors, receive diagnoses, and get treatment recommendations without visiting a clinic or hospital. With telemedicine, you can access healthcare professionals anytime, anywhere, making it a convenient and efficient solution for modern healthcare needs.
